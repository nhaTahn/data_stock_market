## Decision Rule

Step 2A passes if BOTH conditions hold versus `raw_baseline`:
- residual_oracle rel_score mean improvement >= +0.005
- residual_oracle daily_q90_abs_error_q90 mean improvement <= -0.005 (lower is better)

If oracle passes but lagged_ar1 fails, two-stream needs a strong market_pred head.
If oracle fails, the upper bound is exhausted: switch to selective abstention.
